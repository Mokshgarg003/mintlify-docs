# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 2024**

This Privacy Policy explains how Figrfast Systems Private Limited (India) and Figr Design Inc (Delaware, USA), collectively "Figr," collect, use, and protect your information when you use our AI-powered design platform.

<shield>
  **Privacy by design:** We built Figr with privacy as a core principle. We collect only what we need, use it transparently, and give you control over your data.
</shield>

## 1. Information We Collect

**1.1 Account Information**
When you create an account, we collect:

- Name and email address
- Company name and role (optional)
- Billing information (for paid plans)
- Profile preferences and settings

**1.2 Design and Project Data**
When you use our platform, we process:

- Design files and project content you create
- Images, text, and other assets you upload
- Project metadata (names, descriptions, tags)
- Collaboration data (comments, shared links)
- Design system assets and tokens

**1.3 Usage Information**
We automatically collect:

- How you interact with our platform (clicks, navigation patterns)
- Features you use and how frequently
- Performance data (load times, errors)
- Device and browser information
- IP addresses and general location data

**1.4 Communication Data**
We may collect:

- Support requests and our responses
- Feedback and survey responses
- Communication preferences
- Marketing interaction data

## 2. How We Use Your Information

**2.1 Service Provision**
We use your information to:

- Provide and maintain our design platform
- Process your designs using AI technology
- Enable collaboration with your team members
- Sync your data across devices and sessions
- Provide customer support

**2.2 Platform Improvement**
We use aggregated, anonymized data to:

- Improve our AI models and algorithms
- Enhance platform performance and reliability
- Develop new features and capabilities
- Analyze usage patterns and trends
- Conduct research and development

**2.3 Communication**
We may contact you to:

- Send important service updates and security notices
- Respond to your support requests
- Share product updates and new features (if you've opted in)
- Send billing and account information
- Request feedback about our services

**2.4 Legal and Security**
We may use your information to:

- Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms
- Protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats
- Resolve disputes and prevent harmful activities
- Maintain system security and integrity

## 3. AI and Machine Learning

**3.1 AI Training Data**

- We train our AI models using publicly available design resources and patterns
- We do NOT use your private design content to train our AI models
- Your designs remain private and are not incorporated into our training datasets
- We may use anonymized, aggregated usage patterns to improve our algorithms

**3.2 AI Processing**

- Our AI processes your design requests to generate suggestions and content
- This processing happens in real-time and doesn't store your specific design content
- AI-generated content is considered your content under our Terms of Service
- We implement safeguards to prevent AI outputs from inadvertently reproducing others' work

**3.3 Data Security in AI**

- All AI processing occurs on secure, encrypted infrastructure
- We don't share your design content with third-party AI providers
- Our AI models are hosted on our own or carefully vetted partner infrastructure
- We implement data isolation to prevent cross-contamination between users

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

**4.1 We Don't Sell Your Data**

- We never sell your personal information or design data to third parties
- We don't monetize your data through advertising or data broking
- Your creative work remains yours, and we respect your intellectual property

**4.2 Service Providers**
We may share limited data with trusted service providers who help us:

- Provide hosting and infrastructure services
- Process payments and billing
- Deliver customer support
- Perform analytics and security monitoring
- All service providers are bound by strict confidentiality agreements

**4.3 Legal Requirements**
We may disclose information when required to:

- Comply with valid legal processes (subpoenas, court orders)
- Protect our rights, property, or safety
- Prevent fraud or security threats
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- We'll notify you of such requests unless legally prohibited

**4.4 Business Transfers**
If Figr is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets:

- Your data may be transferred as part of that transaction
- We'll notify you before your data is transferred
- The new entity will be bound by this Privacy Policy
- You'll have the option to delete your data before transfer

**4.5 Customer References**
With your use of Figr, you grant us permission to:

- Display your company name and logo as a customer reference
- Include your organization in customer lists and marketing materials
- Use your logo on our website and in promotional content
- You may opt out by contacting [privacy@figr.design](mailto:privacy@figr.design)

## 5. Data Security

**5.1 Security Measures**
We protect your data through:

- Encryption in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- Multi-factor authentication and access controls
- Secure coding practices and vulnerability management
- Employee training on data protection and security

**5.2 Infrastructure Security**

- We use reputable cloud providers with SOC 2 certification
- Data is stored in secure data centers with physical security controls
- We implement network security measures including firewalls and intrusion detection
- Regular backups are encrypted and stored securely
- We maintain incident response procedures for security events

**5.3 Access Controls**

- Access to your data is limited to authorized personnel who need it for their job functions
- We use role-based access controls and the principle of least privilege
- All access is logged and monitored for unusual activity
- Employees undergo background checks and sign confidentiality agreements

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

**6.1 Data Access and Portability**
You have the right to:

- Access all personal data we hold about you
- Export your design files and project data at any time
- Receive your data in a commonly used, machine-readable format
- Transfer your data to another service provider

**6.2 Data Correction and Deletion**
You can:

- Update your account information and preferences
- Correct inaccurate personal data
- Delete your account and associated data
- Request deletion of specific data categories
- We'll process deletion requests within 30 days

**6.3 Communication Preferences**
You may:

- Opt out of marketing communications at any time
- Choose which types of notifications you receive
- Update your communication preferences in your account settings
- Unsubscribe from emails using the links provided

**6.4 Data Processing Objections**
Under applicable law, you may:

- Object to certain types of data processing
- Restrict processing of your personal data
- Withdraw consent for processing based on consent
- File complaints with data protection authorities

## 7. Data Retention

**7.1 Retention Principles**
We retain your data only as long as necessary to:

- Provide our services to you
- Comply with legal obligations
- Resolve disputes and prevent abuse
- Maintain security and system integrity

**7.2 Retention Periods**

- **Account data:** Retained while your account is active, plus 30 days after deletion
- **Design content:** Retained while you maintain the content, deleted within 30 days of user deletion
- **Usage data:** Aggregated and anonymized after 24 months
- **Support communications:** Retained for 3 years for quality assurance
- **Billing records:** Retained for 7 years for legal and tax purposes

**7.3 Automated Deletion**

- Inactive free accounts may be deleted after 2 years of inactivity
- Temporary files and caches are automatically purged regularly
- Backup systems follow the same retention schedules as primary data
- We'll notify you before deleting inactive accounts

## 8. International Data Transfers

**8.1 Global Operations**

- Figr operates globally with servers in multiple regions
- Your data may be processed in countries where our servers or service providers are located
- We ensure adequate protection for international data transfers

**8.2 Transfer Safeguards**
For transfers outside your region, we implement:

- Standard contractual clauses approved by data protection authorities
- Adequacy decisions where available
- Additional safeguards as required by law
- Regular assessments of transfer risks and protections

**8.3 Regional Data Storage**

- EU users' data is primarily stored within the EU
- Indian users' data may be stored in India or other approved locations
- US users' data is primarily stored in the United States
- We honor data localization requirements where applicable

## 9. Children's Privacy

- Our services are not intended for children under 13
- We don't knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
- If we learn we've collected such information, we'll delete it promptly

**9.2 Educational Use**
For educational institutions using Figr:

- Schools must ensure they have appropriate permissions for student use
