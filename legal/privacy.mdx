## Information We Collect

This Privacy Policy covers our treatment of both "Personal Information" and "Device Information" that we collect when you are on our website.

Personal Information includes data like your name, address, and email address that you voluntarily provide when you register or when you contact us directly or via social media.

Device Information is data that we automatically collect about the device you use to access our site. This includes information like what type of device you are using, your device's unique identifiers, your device settings, your operating system, and geo-location data.

## Log Data

When you visit our Service, our servers automatically record information sent from your browser ("Log Data"). This Log Data may include information such as your device’s Internet Protocol (IP) address, browser type and version, the pages of our Service you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages, and other statistics.

Additionally, if an error occurs, we collect data about your device and the error to understand what went wrong to improve our service.

## Device Data

We collect information from and about the device you use to access our Services. This includes things like IP addresses, the type of browser and device you use, the web page you visited before coming to our sites, and identifiers associated with your devices. Your devices (depending on their settings) may also transmit location information to the Services.

## Personal Information

We may ask for personal information, such as your name, email address, physical address, payment information, and social media account details when you register for an account or purchase our products or services. This information is used to fulfill your request, provide relevant information and updates, and for customer service.

## User-Generated Content

Any personal information or content you voluntarily disclose online for the purpose of publication will become publicly available after posting and subsequent review or vetting process. Once published, it may be accessible to third parties not covered under this privacy policy.

## Legitimate Reasons for Processing Your Personal Information

We only collect and use your personal data when we have a legitimate reason for doing so. In which instance, we only collect personal data that is reasonably necessary to provide our Services to you.

When we collect and process personal information, and while we retain this information, we will protect it within commercially acceptable means to prevent loss and theft, as well as unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use, or modification

Although we will do our best to protect the personal information you provide to us, we advise that no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and no one can guarantee absolute data security.

You are responsible for selecting any password and its overall security strength, ensuring the security of your own information within the bounds of our services. For example, ensuring any passwords associated with accessing your personal information and accounts are secure and confidential.

_Consent for Marketing_: If you have previously given your consent to use your personal data for our marketing initiatives, you have the right to withdraw this consent anytime by contacting us. _Filing Complaints_: If you feel that we have violated a relevant data protection law and wish to lodge a complaint, please contact us with full details of the alleged breach. We will promptly

investigate your complaint and respond to you in writing, explaining the results of our investigation and the steps we will take to address your complaint.
