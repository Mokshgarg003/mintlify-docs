Credits power every generation in Figr. Open **Earn more credits** at the bottom of the left sidebar to see your tasks and claim rewards.

<Frame caption="The Earn Credits panel, opened from the sidebar.">
  ![Earn Credits](/images/earn-credits.png)
</Frame>

## Ways to earn

| Task | What you do | Reward |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Set up your design system | Connect Figma or upload files so Figr learns your tokens | 5 credits |
| Install the Chrome extension | Capture live products from the web as input | 5 credits |
| Review on G2 or Product Hunt | Share honest feedback about Figr | 5 credits |
| Share on X or LinkedIn | Post about Figr and claim your credits | 5 credits |

Each task credits your balance once. Set up your design system from the [Design Language](/product-intelligence/design-system) page; the rest are one click from the Earn more credits panel.

## Affiliate program

Refer PMs, designers, and SaaS teams and earn 20% recurring commission for as long as they stay subscribed. Your referral link, commissions, and payouts live in Rewardful. [Become an affiliate](https://figr.design/affiliate-program).
