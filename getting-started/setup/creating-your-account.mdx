# Creating Your Account

Getting started with Figr takes less than 3 minutes. Here's everything you need to know about account creation and initial setup.

<Callout type="tip">
**Pro tip:** Have your design system files and product URLs ready. You can upload them during setup to get immediate value from Figr's product-aware features.
</Callout>

## Sign Up Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Figr">
    Go to [app.figr.design](https://app.figr.design) and click **"Get Started"**
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/signup-landing.png" alt="Figr landing page with Get Started button highlighted" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Your Sign-Up Method">
    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Email & Password">
        - Enter your work email address
        - Create a secure password (8+ characters)
        - Verify your email address
      </Tab>
      
      <Tab title="Google SSO">
        - Click "Continue with Google"
        - Select your Google account
        - Authorize Figr access
      </Tab>
      
      <Tab title="GitHub SSO">
        - Click "Continue with GitHub"  
        - Authorize Figr access
        - Perfect for engineering teams
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Account Information">
    Tell us about yourself:
    
    ```
    Full Name: Your professional name
    Company: Your organization name
    Role: Designer/PM/Engineer/Founder/Other
    Team Size: 1-5/6-20/21-100/100+
    ```
    
    <Callout type="info">
    This information helps Figr customize the experience for your role and team size.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Email Verification">
    - Check your email for verification link
    - Click the link to activate your account
    - You'll be redirected to your dashboard
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Security & Privacy Settings

Configure your account security from **Settings > Security**:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Password & Authentication">
    - **Change Password:** Update your login credentials
    - **Two-Factor Authentication:** Add SMS or authenticator app 2FA
    - **SSO Management:** Connect or disconnect SSO providers
    
    <Callout type="warning">
    **Highly recommended:** Enable 2FA to protect your design assets and product data.
    </Callout>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Data & Privacy">
    - **Data Retention:** Control how long Figr stores your designs
    - **Analytics Opt-out:** Disable usage analytics collection
    - **Export Data:** Download all your designs and memory data
    - **Delete Account:** Permanently remove your account and data
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Integrations">
    - **Connected Apps:** Manage third-party tool connections
    - **API Keys:** Generate keys for custom integrations
    - **Webhooks:** Set up notifications for design updates
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Common Setup Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Email Verification Not Received">
    **Solutions:**
    1. Check spam/junk folders
    2. Add `noreply@figr.design` to contacts
    3. Request new verification email from login page
    4. Contact support if issue persists
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="SSO Login Issues">
    **Solutions:**
    1. Ensure your organization has approved Figr
    2. Try clearing browser cookies and cache
    3. Use private/incognito browser window
    4. Contact your IT admin for permission issues
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Payment Method Declined">
    **Solutions:**
    1. Verify card details are correct
    2. Check with bank for international transaction blocks
    3. Try alternative payment method
    4. Contact billing support for assistance
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
