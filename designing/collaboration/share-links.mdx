# Share Links

Share your designs securely with stakeholders, clients, and team members through customizable sharing links. Control access, gather feedback, and maintain design confidentiality.

<info>
  **Secure collaboration:** Share designs with the right people, at the right time, with the right level of access - all while maintaining security and control over your design assets.
</info>

  <Accordion title="Performance Issues">
