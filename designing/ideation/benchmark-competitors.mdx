# Benchmark Competitors

Competitive analysis fuels better design decisions. Figr helps you systematically analyze competitor products, identify best practices, and discover opportunities for differentiation.

<Callout type="info">
**Strategic advantage:** Understanding the competitive landscape helps you design solutions that stand out while meeting user expectations established by industry leaders.
</Callout>

## Ethical Competitive Analysis

<Callout type="warning">
**Responsible research:** Conduct competitive analysis ethically and legally, respecting intellectual property and using only publicly available information.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Legal Boundaries">
    **Stay within legal limits:**
    
    ```yaml
    Acceptable practices:
    ‚úÖ Public product testing and screenshots
    ‚úÖ Published marketing materials analysis
    ‚úÖ User review and feedback analysis
    ‚úÖ Industry report references
    ‚úÖ Conference presentation insights
    
    Avoid:
    ‚ùå Proprietary information access
    ‚ùå Terms of service violations
    ‚ùå Unauthorized account access
    ‚ùå IP infringement
    ‚ùå Employee information gathering
    ```
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Inspiration vs Copying">
    **Use insights appropriately:**
    
    ```yaml
    Good practice:
    ‚úÖ Learn from successful patterns
    ‚úÖ Understand user expectations
    ‚úÖ Identify improvement opportunities
    ‚úÖ Validate design approaches
    ‚úÖ Build on industry standards
    
    Avoid:
    ‚ùå Direct copying of designs

    ‚ùå Violating design patents
    ```

  </Step>
</Steps>
